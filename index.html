Investigation & Research

Understanding the core difficulties students face when preparing for high-stakes testing.

Community Need

Studying is notoriously difficult but absolutely necessary to do well on tests. We identified that the primary stakeholders facing this challenge are students and teachers who need better tools for comprehension.

Root Causes

Many existing studying solutions are simply not sufficient for difficult tests. They often promote rote memorization over actual understanding, leaving students unprepared for complex problem-solving.

The Evidence

Research shows a critical gap in educational success:

20-40%
of students fail at least one class in high school.

Innovation & Action

The Solution

Study Buddy is an AI study helper that walks alongside you. Instead of just acting as an answer key, it functions as a digital tutor to help you learn the material you are studying instead of just memorizing it.

Technical Highlights

"The core functionality is the AI being taught that, as opposed to giving answers, it helps you through the question so that you can better learn."

Impact & Reflection

Social Impact

Study Buddy provides crucial help to kids struggling to learn and get ready for tests. While the immediate impact scales person-by-person, it is highly significant: because some people are simply not naturally good test takers, a deeper, guided learning of the information can help them successfully get through school.

Personal Growth

"This showed me the difficulty of making something like this, as well as the importance of better understanding for some people who struggle more in a testing environment."

Future Scalability

I think if I had another semester, it would be nice to have it watch your paper while you work, and if it sees you make mistakes it corrects you on the spot or after you say you're done. This could help the AI give better help, since instead of it trying to figure out what you did just based on your words, it knows what you did and what you did wrong.
