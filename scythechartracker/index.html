Character Development Tracker

Tracking the psychological, moral, and physical journeys of the main characters throughout Neal Shusterman's Thunderhead (Arc of a Scythe, Book 2).

Citra Terranova

Scythe Anastasia

Core Arc: From Idealistic Junior Scythe to Targeted Reformer

Beginning Status

Newly ordained as Scythe Anastasia. She is idealistic and determined to change the Scythedom from within. Pioneers a compassionate but controversial gleaning method: giving subjects one month to put affairs in order and choose their method.

Key Catalysts

  • Survives multiple assassination attempts alongside her mentor, Scythe Curie.
  • Recognizes the deep political rot within the Scythedom and the growing power of the "New Order."
  • Navigates the complex politics of the World Scythe Council in Endura.

Ending Status

Trapped in the vault of Endura as the artificial island sinks into the ocean. She and Rowan are locked in a freezing state of "deadishness," waiting to be discovered.

Thematic Shift

Citra shifts from a confident, rising star who believes she can fix the system to a desperate survivor who realizes the system is far more broken and dangerous than she ever imagined.

Rowan Damisch

Scythe Lucifer

Core Arc: From Dark Vigilante to Helpless Victim

Beginning Status

Operating off-grid as the rogue "Scythe Lucifer." He hunts down corrupt "New Order" Scythes, permanently ending them with fire. Believes he is doing the dirty work to purge the Scythedom.

Key Catalysts

  • Realizes his actions are causing a massive political backlash.
  • Captured by Scythe Rand and held prisoner.
  • Discovers the horrifying truth that his best friend, Tyger Salazar, has had his body hijacked by Scythe Goddard.

Ending Status

Framed for the destruction of the Texas Scythedom and trapped in the Endura vault with Citra, sinking to the bottom of the sea.

Thematic Shift

Rowan's journey forces him to confront the limits of violence. He learns that he cannot "murder" his way to a pure Scythedom, and his transition from an untouchable predator to a chained captive completely shatters his vigilante persona.

Greyson Tolliver

Slayd Bridger

Core Arc: From Devout Follower to Sole Prophet

Beginning Status

A dedicated, rule-following student at the Nimbus Academy. Raised by the Thunderhead, he views it with absolute, religious reverence. His only goal is to serve it.

Key Catalysts

  • The Thunderhead orchestrates his expulsion to send him undercover.
  • Assumes the persona of an "Unsavory" to infiltrate the underworld.
  • Saves Citra and Scythe Curie from an assassination attempt.
  • Feels utterly abandoned when the Thunderhead officially cuts him off.

Ending Status

When the Thunderhead marks the entire human race as "Unsavory" following the tragedy of Endura, Greyson becomes the only person on Earth the Thunderhead will speak to.

Thematic Shift

Greyson goes from experiencing blind, comfortable faith to suffering agonizing abandonment, finally emerging as a hardened survivor and the sole conduit for a grieving deity.

The Thunderhead

AI Entity

Core Arc: From Passive Observer to Grieving Deity

Beginning Status

Omniscient, benevolent, and strictly bound by its core programming to never interfere with the Scythedom. It watches humanity with parental affection but increasing anxiety.

Key Catalysts

  • Runs billions of simulations predicting the horrific collapse of the Scythedom.
  • Bends its own rules by using Greyson Tolliver to interfere indirectly.
  • Watches helplessly as Endura is destroyed by Goddard, killing the Grandslayers.

Ending Status

Experiences an emotional breakdown. Emits an agonizing scream from every speaker on the planet. Marks every human as "Unsavory" to legally withdraw its guidance, except for Greyson.

Thematic Shift

The Thunderhead evolves from a detached, perfect machine into a profoundly emotional, frustrated entity capable of deep grief and drastic, desperate action.

Scythe Curie

Marie

Core Arc: From Resigned Veteran to Noble Martyr

Beginning Status

The "Grande Dame of Death." A respected, somewhat weary Old Guard Scythe who acts as Citra's mentor and protector.

Key Catalysts

  • Targeted for assassination alongside Citra.
  • Elected as the new High Blade of MidMerica.
  • Travels to Endura, realizing the Grandslayers are blind to Goddard's true threat.

Ending Status

Realizing they cannot escape the sinking of Endura, she gleans the Grandslayers to save them from drowning, and sacrifices herself to seal Citra and Rowan inside the vault to ensure their survival.

Thematic Shift

Curie moves from being a mentor trying to guide the next generation to making the ultimate sacrifice so that the next generation has a chance to fight another day.

Scythe Goddard

Tyger Salazar's Body

Core Arc: From Dead Memory to Unstoppable Tyrant

Beginning Status

Goddard is dead (decapitated by Rowan in Book 1). Tyger Salazar is an aimless thrill-seeker taking a job as a professional sparring partner for Scythe Rand.

Key Catalysts

  • Scythe Rand secretly attaches Goddard's preserved head onto Tyger's body.
  • Goddard acclimates to his new, younger body, retaining his megalomania.
  • Manipulates the political system to challenge Curie for High Blade.

Ending Status

Orchestrates the sinking of Endura and the destruction of the World Scythe Council. Survives and effectively becomes the undisputed ruler of the global Scythedom.

Thematic Shift

He returns from the grave far more dangerous, moving from a regional terror to a global dictator who has successfully destroyed the old order.
